Introduction to My Favourite Mobile Legends Characters



Alucard is a demon hunter with a deep hatred for demons. He's a Fighter/Assassin hybrid meaning he can be played as a side laner.

Lesley, the Deadly Sniper, is a hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang. Lesley is one of the few marksmen in the game who use Energy instead of Mana.

Lunox was born with magical powers, and her dreamworld reflects and changes the real world.

Chou is a gifted martial artist born in the slums. He learned the ancient oriental martial arts under the tutelage of a mysterious monk.
